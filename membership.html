Membership

Join ND TSA — opportunities for competition, leadership, and career exploration.

Become a Member

Register your chapter and members through the TSA membership portal. For chapter-level registration use the link below. Individual chapters and students should coordinate with their local CTSO advisor.

Chapter registration: tsamembership.registermychapter.com

Benefits of membership include:

  • Local, state, and national competitive events
  • Career awareness opportunities
  • 21st century leadership skills activities
  • Technology literacy development
  • National service project
  • Networking activities
  • Scholarship and award opportunities
