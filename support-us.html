About TSA

The Technology Student Association (TSA)

TSA is a national non-profit CTSO of middle and high school students engaged in STEM. Over 300,000 students participate in competitions, leadership opportunities, and community service.

North Dakota TSA

  • Partners with businesses and organizations
  • Offers statewide conferences and events
